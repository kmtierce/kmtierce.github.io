During the 90's I was only part of one group, Wild West. Wild West was pretty short lived, and I was only a side man in it. I left the group to work on my own tunes and do some solo recording, and I called this project Mighty Mambo (translation: big dance). Consisting of upbeat, straight-ahead rock and blues music, Mighty Mambo is my favorite ongoing project, and the best fit for most of my writing.
